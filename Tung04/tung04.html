Tungurahua, Ecuador
Experiments on Tungurahua Volcano, one of the most active and dangerous volcanoes in the Northern Andes. I collaborate with researchers at the Instituto Geofisico Escuela Politecnica Nacional, Quito, and especially Professor Mario Ruiz.

See these papers for details:
  • Ruiz, M, J. M. Lees, and J. B. Johnson (2005), Source constraints of Tungurahua explosion events, Bulletin of Volcanology doi:DOI 10.1007/s00445-005-0023-8
  • Werner-Allen, G., J. Johnson, M. Ruiz, J. Lees, M. Welsh (2005) Monitoring Volcanic Eruptions with a Wireless Sensor Network. in Proc. Second European Workshop on Wireless Sensor Networks (EWSN'05 #1568945184).
